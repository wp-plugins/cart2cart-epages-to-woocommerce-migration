== Description ==

Cart2Cart: ePages to WooCommerce Migration plugin is one of the most simplest and effective ways to transfer your e-store data from one platform to another. With the help of additional options, you can customize migration settings, adjusting it to your needs. Transferring process itself, after setting a connection between stores and choosing entities, doesn’t require user interference and goes fully automatically. The plugin is absolutely free to download and install, though migration itself is paid. The price variates up from $69 and depends on the amount of transferring entities and additional options. 

= Features =
* **No Downtime.** Your ePages store will be online during the whole migration process. 
* **Free Demo Migration.** Try out migration plugin and transfer up to 10 entities for free.
* **No Tech Skills Required.** Cart2Cart migration plugin allows to perform switch without any need of professional assistance. 
* **Qualified Support.** Cart2Cart Support Team is always ready to give a hand and answer your questions. 

**Transfer the following data from ePages to WooCommerce:**

* Products, product images, product attributes, product variants;
* Categories, category images;
* Customers, customer shipping address, customer billing address;
* Orders, order statuses, order products;
* Manufacturers.

*Supported WooCommerce versions:* 1.1.x - 1.6.x, 2.x
**Please note:** Your ePages design will not be migrated, as Cart2Cart transfers data only.

Cart2Cart: ePages to WooCommerce Migration plugin is a simple but effective solution, that allows you to platform switch on your own. First of all, you have to download the migration plugin. Then, install it and establish the connection between your stores. Afterwards, you’ll be redirected to Cart2Cart website in order to continue migration. To ensure that everything has been set properly, choose free Demo Migration option, that will move up to 10 entities to WooCommerce in terms of minutes. Finally, select Insurance plan (not necessary, but recommended) and launch Full Migration to start automated data transfer. 

**Pre-Migration Steps**

1. Install WooCommerce store and make sure it can be reached via web 

== Installation ==

1. Download plugin.zip file and extract it to your hard drive
1. Upload unzipped file to wp-content/plugin directory
1. Activate “Cart2Cart ePages to WooCommerce Migration” in plugins section of your admin panel
1. Insert your current ePages store link into an appropriate field  
1. Connection Bridge files for WooCommerce are will be uploaded automatically
1. Click Start Migration button and follow the Migration Wizard to perform accurate and swift migration

== Frequently Asked Questions ==
= Will customers' passwords be migrated? =
As diverse platforms use different password encryption algorithm, clients’ passwords cannot be migrated. Once the transfer process is finished all registered clients will have to use password recovery form on the target platform for further usage their profiles.
= Does migration influence the speed of my store? =
The migration process doesn’t influence the speed of your store performance. Needless, to say a newly established store without data will work faster than a store with thousands of products, orders and customers.
= Can I migrate product and category SEO URLs? =
Yes, you can migrate product and category SEO URLs from ePages to WooCommerce. To do this, you need to choose it in Additional Options. 
= Will my current ePages function during the migration? =
Migration process won't affect your source shopping cart in any way. It will be up and running successfully during and after the migration process.
